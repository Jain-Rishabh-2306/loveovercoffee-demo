Our Story

Where every cup is brewed with love

Born From a Love of Coffee

Love Over Coffee was born from a simple idea — that great coffee and great company can make any moment special. Nestled in the heart of Jagatpura, we set out to create a space where people could slow down, connect, and enjoy something truly delicious.

From our very first cup, we believed that coffee is more than just a drink — it's a conversation starter, a comfort, a ritual. That belief drives everything we do, from the beans we source to the way we craft every single order.

Whether you're here for a first date, a catch-up with friends, a quick work break, or just a late-night coffee — Love Over Coffee is your place. We're more than a cafe. We're Jagatpura's favorite hangout.
